Jo Butler
When Jo set out to find a cheaper and more private solution for her own personal tanning, she never realised how one decision could set up her own personal business populating an ever growing cliental base. What started as a tent in her bathroom 3 years ago trying different products that suited her and her daughter has now giving her years of practice on family and friends insuring she now gives a professional finish to each of her clients with the best current products.

Word of mouth got around after her happy clients referred their friends onto Jo expressing they would not go anywhere else, not just because of the products and services she gives, but the privacy and convenience of bookings outside of work hours.

Tanjo was officially born in 2022 after its client base grew so much that she has had to give up her full time job as a Stop Smoking Practitioner in order to now keep up with the high demand of bookings across Canterbury. Jo uses all her own branded products that come in colours Tanjo Light (for naturally dark skin tones or anyone that would just like a light tan), Tanjo Medium (for clients with naturally medium skin tones) and Tanjo Dark (for those with naturally fair skin that would like to go dark). All products are 100% cruelty free, 100% DHA, Paraben, palm oil, mineral oil & sulfate free, bottles are made from recyclable materials and sustainably sourced!

Simply for booking all you need to do is go to the booking page here on the site, enter all required fields and submit! Once you have submitted you will be prompted a thank you message and a link to return to prior page. Your booking will then go through to the Tanjo Tanning Inbox where Jo will respond to your enquiry about availability of that time or confirmation on your booking within 3 hours! All you need to do is allocate 30 minutes from start to finish of tan, arrive in loose clothing, make sure you have the correct cash amount and be on time. We cant wait to see you!
