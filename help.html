Great Question! Sending a booking request to Tanjo is super easy! All you need to do is click the three line menu toggle at the top left of the screen, go down the menu to "Bookings" and on that page is a simple Booking Request Form. Simply fill out all your details that are required on the form, the press "Send Request". Your page will come up with a confirmation page from "GetForm" to confirm your message has been sent through to Tanjos. Tanjo's will access your booking request, compare it with their bookings for the date and time you have requested and send a confirmation email back to the email you provided in your submitted form within 3 hours.
By chance if your requested time is already taken, you will be emailed with a time closest to when you requested.
